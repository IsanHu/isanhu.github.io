Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

This policy explains how 1318 Studio handles information for Timestamps for Chatbots, a Safari extension that displays local timestamps on supported chatbot websites.

Data collection

Timestamps for Chatbots does not collect, sell, share, or transmit personal data to 1318 Studio. The extension does not use analytics, advertising trackers, or a remote 1318 Studio server.

Local page processing

To show timestamps, the extension runs locally in Safari on supported chatbot websites. It inspects page content and page responses only to find message timestamp information and render that timestamp in the page. This processing happens on your device.

Local storage

The extension stores only your settings in Safari extension storage on your device, including your preferred time format and which supported chatbot sites are enabled. These settings are not sent to 1318 Studio.

Third-party websites

Supported chatbot websites are operated by their own providers. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies, not this policy.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new last updated date.
